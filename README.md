# Pharma Analytics Dashboard - Power BI

## 📘 Project Overview
This repository contains the **Pharma Analytics Dashboard**, developed in Power BI.  
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of **hospital performance and pharmaceutical sales**, combining clinical, operational, and financial insights.  
It is designed to support healthcare leaders and pharma executives in making data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- **Healthcare Analytics:** Track patient demographics, admission trends, treatment costs, and departmental performance.  
- **Pharmaceutical Sales Analysis:** Evaluate product sales, distributor performance, and market distribution.  
- **Financial Insights:** Identify cost variations across departments and states, and monitor overall sales growth.  
- **Strategic Decision Support:** Provide actionable insights for hospital management and pharmaceutical companies.

---

## 📊 Key Highlights

### Healthcare Analytics
- **10,000 patients** analyzed across multiple departments.  
- **Most common diagnosis:** Migraine.  
- **Most expensive medicine:** Lisinopril.  
- **33.58%** of medicines discontinued.  
- Departmental distribution: Cardiology (18.46%), Neurology (20.68%), Orthopedics (20.92%), Pediatrics (21.7%), General Medicine (18.24%).  
- Average treatment costs range from **$516.33 (Orthopedics)** to **$532.50 (Neurology)**.  
- Patient demographics segmented by age and gender.

### Pharmaceutical Sales Analysis
- **Total sales:** $11.80 billion.  
- **Top product:** Ionclotide ($169M).  
- **Top product class:** Analgesics.  
- **Top city by sales:** Butzbach.  
- Sales growth from **2017–2020**, reaching **$3.5B in 2020**.  
- Distribution channels: Retail (21.38%), Government (28.33%), Private (25.92%), Institution (24.37%).  
- Top 10 products include Ionclotide, Tetratanyl, Sumanazole, Betanem, and others.
